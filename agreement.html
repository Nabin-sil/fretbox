Onboarding Agreement - Housing communities

We are happy that you have decided to opt for our services. We are glad to have you in our FretBox family. FretBox is on its mission to see happy people around by improving communication, and easing the processes in housing societies. To make this mission successful, we work together with you as a team. To start, you need to provide us permission for the points mentioned below

  • Permission to talk& train to the people

    • As a part of our training, we might need to talk to society people to explain our product & explain how to use. People here include society residents as well as society employees (Security guards, manager, committee Etc.) During the training period (Normally 2-4 days), FretBox executives will be present in your society premises or provide online training for remote installation. You hereby give us permission to be present in your society meeting (will be informed to us in advance by society management).

  • Permission for awareness campaign

    • To make residents aware about our product & effective communication, you hereby give us permission to put our banner on the society main gate & pamphlets/ user guide on the society notice boards. FretBox executives might visit residents home to help them download the app & explain our product.

  • Confirming Onboarding of FretBox

    • By signing this document, you give us permission to talk to people, trained people, conduct awareness campaign and agree to our terms and conditions &accept our privacy policy to on board FretBox & use our product & services for your society operations management.
